Like most of the pre-Flight Miasma material, the text here is written in crude iron gall ink, consistently dated with the Eremite's outward journey, especially the years spent gyring. The binding and thus the ordering of the pages has been lost along with the complete folio, but we are used to such by now. The illegibility of the script and wave of the line, unusual in the Miasma Eremite's oeuvre, is consistent with his delicate state of health and the turbulence associated with that particular stretch of water.
